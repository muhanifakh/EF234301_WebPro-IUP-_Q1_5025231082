Delicious Foods of Surabaya

Surabaya is renowned for its unique and diverse culinary scene. Here, you can find a variety of traditional dishes that are rich in flavor and history. Let’s dive into some of the must-try foods that define Surabaya’s vibrant food culture!

Semanggi

A local favorite made from steamed clover leaves, served with a flavorful peanut sauce and crispy crackers. This dish is a true taste of Surabaya's heritage.

Lontong Balap

A hearty dish consisting of rice cakes, bean sprouts, tofu, and a savory broth. It's often served with 'lentho' (fried mashed beans) and adds a burst of flavor to your palate.

Tahu Campur

A delightful mix of tofu, vegetables, noodles, and tender beef slices, all topped with a savory sauce made from fermented shrimp paste. It’s a perfect blend of flavors and textures.

Tips for Enjoying Surabaya's Cuisine

  • Explore local markets like Pasar Atom or Pasar Genteng to find the freshest ingredients and experience authentic street food.
  • Try pairing your meals with 'Es Dawet' or 'Es Campur,' two popular refreshing drinks in Surabaya.
  • Don’t be afraid to try something new – the more adventurous you are with the dishes, the more you'll appreciate the diverse flavors!

Where to Find the Best Foods in Surabaya

If you're eager to taste these dishes, here are some recommended spots:

  • G-Walk: A popular night market offering a variety of street food options.
  • Taman Bungkul: Known as one of the best places to try Semanggi, this park offers a variety of local delicacies.
  • Depot Asli Lontong Balap: Famous for its Lontong Balap, this small eatery is a must-visit for food enthusiasts.
