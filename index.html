Welcome to the city of Heroes!

Surabaya is not just a city; it's a cultural melting pot with a rich history and vibrant lifestyle. From historic landmarks to modern attractions, this city offers a unique blend of experiences that are both exciting and inspiring

Savor the Flavors of Surabaya

Discover the unique tastes of Surabaya through its iconic dishes: Semanggi, Lontong Balap, and Tahu Campur. Each dish offers a distinct blend of flavors, from the savory peanut sauce of Semanggi to the rich broth of Lontong Balap, and the hearty mix of Tahu Campur. Experience the culinary traditions that make Surabaya's cuisine truly unforgettable.

Suro & Boyo Statue

It wouldn't feel complete visiting Surabaya without taking photos with the shark and crocodile statue in the background. There are several spots to capture photos with the Suro and Boyo statue. One of the best spots is from the Skateboard Park.

Kelenteng Sanggar Agung

Located in the Ria Kenjeran Beach area, Kelenteng Sanggar Agung has its own unique charm. Here, you’ll find many statues such as the Goddess Kwan Im, which is the main icon of this temple, along with the statues of Sha Nan and Tong Nu, as well as a winding dragon statue.

Tugu Pahlawan

A tourist spot in Surabaya that you shouldn't miss is, the Heroes Monument (Tugu Pahlawan). This monument is a very popular icon of Surabaya. The Heroes Monument is surrounded by lush greenery, making it a perfect place to relax after exploring the city of Surabaya.
